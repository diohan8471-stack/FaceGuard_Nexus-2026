━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  PART 1 — UPLOAD CODE TO GITHUB
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1. Go to https://github.com on your phone
Step 2. Sign up or log in
Step 3. Tap the ➕ icon → "New repository"
Step 4. Name it: faceguard-nexus
Step 5. Set to PUBLIC (required for free Render)
Step 6. Tap "Create repository"

Step 7. On the next page, tap "uploading an existing file"
Step 8. Upload ALL files from this folder:
          app.py
          requirements.txt
          Procfile
          render.yaml
          runtime.txt
          .gitignore
          static/  (folder with style.css, manifest.json)
          templates/ (folder with all .html files)

  ⚠️  You can upload folders by dragging them in on a computer.
      On phone: upload files one by one or use GitHub Desktop app.

Step 9. Tap "Commit changes"
Step 10. Your code is now on GitHub ✅

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  PART 2 — DEPLOY ON RENDER (free hosting)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1. Go to https://render.com
Step 2. Sign up with your GitHub account (same account!)
Step 3. Click "New +" → "Web Service"
Step 4. Connect your GitHub → select "faceguard-nexus"
Step 5. Fill in these settings:
          Name:           faceguard-nexus (or anything)
          Runtime:        Python 3
          Build Command:  pip install -r requirements.txt
          Start Command:  gunicorn app:app --workers 2 --bind 0.0.0.0:$PORT --timeout 120
          Plan:           FREE ✅

Step 6. Scroll down to "Environment Variables" → Add:
          Key: SECRET_KEY
          Value: (click "Generate" button — Render will make one)

          Key: PRODUCTION
          Value: true

          Key: DB_PATH
          Value: /opt/render/project/src/nexus.db

Step 7. Scroll to "Disk" section → Add Disk:
          Name:       nexus-data
          Mount Path: /opt/render/project/src
          Size:       1 GB (free)

  ⚠️  THE DISK IS CRITICAL. Without it, your database
      gets wiped every time the server restarts!

Step 8. Click "Create Web Service"
Step 9. Wait 3–5 minutes for it to build and deploy
Step 10. Render gives you a URL like:
           https://faceguard-nexus.onrender.com

Your app is now LIVE on the internet! ✅
Everyone can access it from any device. 🎉

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  PART 3 — FIRST TIME SETUP (after deploy)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1. Open your Render URL in Chrome
Step 2. You'll be redirected to /apply (no users yet)
Step 3. Go to YOUR_URL/register instead
Step 4. Fill your info — set Role Type to ADMIN
Step 5. Take 5 face photos → Register
Step 6. You are now the admin!
Step 7. Go to /login → scan your face → you're in

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  PART 4 — HOW OTHERS JOIN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Option A — Share the apply link:
  Send people to: https://YOUR_APP.onrender.com/apply
  They fill their info + take face photos → Submit
  You approve or reject from /pending

Option B — Use invite links (more secure):
  Go to /settings → Invite Links → Generate
  Share that specific link to specific people only
  Disable the link when done

  IMPORTANT NOTES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

⚠️  FREE RENDER TIER SLEEPS after 15 minutes of no traffic.
    First visit after sleep takes ~30 seconds to wake up.
    This is normal. Upgrade to paid ($7/mo) to keep it awake.

⚠️  HTTPS is automatic on Render.
    Camera (face login) requires HTTPS and works fine.

⚠️  YOUR DATABASE lives at /opt/render/project/src/nexus.db
    The 1GB disk keeps it safe across restarts.
    Always export a backup from /settings regularly.

⚠️  IF YOU UPDATE THE CODE:
    Push the change to GitHub.
    Render auto-deploys in ~2 minutes.
